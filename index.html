Story Of Marlee

On Sunday, December 28, Kaylee and Jasons home in Washington, DC was the site of an unimaginable tragedy. While they were out of town, their home was broken into, robbed, vandalized, and intentionally set on fire. Kaylee and Jasonboth active-duty members of the U.S. Space Forceand their beautiful baby girl, Rae, lost pretty much everything they owned. From clothing and furniture to baby items, keepsakes, and essentialsnothing was spared. Tragically, in the fire they also lost their beloved cat, Marlee, who was a cherished part of their family. The emotional toll of this loss, on top of the destruction of their home, is overwhelming. We are incredibly grateful that Kaylee, Jason, and Rae were not home at the time and are physically safe. However, they are now faced with the daunting task of starting over completely from nothing.
